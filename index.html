ML Strength is a fitness company that was founded in 2010 by Mark Lewis, a former professional bodybuilder and fitness trainer. The company's main focus is on providing high-quality fitness services to people of all ages and fitness levels.

We has a strong commitment to promoting healthy lifestyles and helping clients achieve their fitness goals through personalized training and nutrition plans. The company has grown steadily since its founding and has become a well-respected name in the fitness industry, known for its knowledgeable trainers, state-of-the-art facilities, and friendly, welcoming atmosphere.
